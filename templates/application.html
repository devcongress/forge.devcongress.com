Learn

At Forge we encourage individuals to come in knowing what they want to be good at and what they want to learn.

We will give you the necessary tools and the environment for you to master whatever you want to be good at.

Build your skills

When you have nothing to worry about, when the world unfolds before you in a controlled manner, when the world is predictive, you can only exercise your fore-knowledge to build your character in such a way to be significant every step of the process. At Forge your time will be spent building your programming skill. That is the most important thing.

Do you want to contribute to an open source project? Here at Forge you can spend your time digging into the internals of the project, learning better programming practices along the way. A huge part of your knowledge base would come from reading other people's code. We believe (and we think the world does too) that there's no better way to be a better programming than reading good code.

Contribute

By this time you're ready to add to the goodness in this world. Go on and contribute your heart's content to all the open source projects that need you. (And we think all open source projects are open to contributors.) Do you want to start your own open source project? Do you want to contribute to your local society?

Or go ahead and get a job and help your company inch closer to achieving their honorable missions.

Up your software engineering skills in 12 weeks

Forge is a full-time 12-week intensive programme that is designed to help you improve your software engineering skills. Over twelve weeks, you'll learn the neccessary skills needed to make you a software engineer. You will learn computer science fundamentals and software engineering best practices

Learn from the experts

Our instructors are individuals who are in the software industry. They give students real world practical education and take hands-on approach to teaching.
