What is FORGE?

FORGE is the place where rare hackers are FORGED! It is an intense anvil on which only the brave and best are born, crafted and carefully FORGEd into immaculate hacking species. Three months is just the beginning of the new and better you. If you go back the same then we have failed. We will pay you for the three months of your life’s worth.

Why should I go to FORGE?

You don’t have to go to FORGE unless you are an exceptional person, driven, intelligent and determined to make a difference with your life: a difference women will sing about and dance at full moons, parents will name their children after you, and children will read about in school. If you are not that kind, then go on and live your sorry life. We are in the business of making history. Now move over! But if you are then prove it.

What are the pre-requisites?

First of all you should be insane. Insane enough to believe that your can transform your life in 3 months. Second you should be hungry and foolish. So hungry that you will make the most of any given chance and will beat your best performance ever. And be foolish enough to commit your life to that end. Thirdly, you should have evidence to prove you really want this: have programming knowledge and worked on something significant before.(Git/BitBucket is your witness).
